Bloomstar Bouquet history

A brief history of the company. Bloomstar is a flower retail company that was founded in 2001 by Succar family. In 2015 Bloomstar acquired Ecuador farms, in 2016 it was time to be acquired by Elite Flowers which also has operations in the United States of America (USA), and in 2020 Bloomstar acquired Valley Flowers. Since it was founded Bloomstar grew from a small family-owned flower retail business to one of the biggest flower distributors in Canada. The rapid growth came side by side with the necessity of collecting, storing and analyzing information from their clients along with internal data related to production and sales.

Details about the flower market
