payment & ordering

Bloomstar is currently changing the way it sells its products. Instead of physical locations, we will be developing an online store.


It will not be necessary for you as a customer to get out of your comfy house to find a physical store to buy the flowers that you are looking for. With just a click of a button, you will be able to:


  • Look at our online flower catalogue.


  • Define with flower, color and quantity you want.


  • Choose where it is going to be delivered. We deliver all over Canada.

Our e-commerce will only store basic information about our clients. We never ask for or store confidential data from our customers.


We work with the best third-party payment companies in the market like PayPal, Square and Stripe.


  • With just a few clicks and some basic information, you can buy the most beautiful flowers on the internet.


  • We are a serious company that puts our customers first. What are you waiting for to start your order?
