handling & delivery

We handle all our products with care and we guarantee delivery on time.


No matter where you are in Canada our flowers are always going to be delivered to you fresh and clean.


  • Our products are always fresh because we have at least three warehouses in each Canadian province. This indicates our commitment to our clients.


  • Our highly trained drivers are working 24/7/365 to make sure all customers will get their products on time. In some cases, if the order is urgent, a third-party logistics company will take care of your delivery, but rest assured this will not change the freshness of your flowers.
